Section 1

Definition of Hajj

朝觐的定义

Hajj (الحج) literally means "to make a resolve to visit a holy place." It refers to the annual pilgrimage to the holy city of Makkah (Mecca), Saudi Arabia, performed during the Islamic month of Dhul-Hijjah (ذو الحجة) — the 12th month of the Islamic calendar. Hajj is the Fifth Pillar of Islam, obligatory upon every sane, adult Muslim who is financially and physically capable, at least once in their lifetime.

朝觐（الحج）字面意思是"下定决心前往圣地"。它指每年在伊斯兰历十二月（朝觐月）前往沙特阿拉伯麦加圣城进行的年度朝圣。朝觐是伊斯兰五功的第五功，是每一位具有理智、已达青春期、且在经济和体力上有能力的穆斯林，一生中至少必须履行一次的义务。

وَلِلَّهِ عَلَى النَّاسِ حِجُّ الْبَيْتِ مَنِ اسْتَطَاعَ إِلَيْهِ سَبِيلًا

"And [due] to Allah from the people is a pilgrimage to the House — for whoever is able to find thereto a way."

"凡能前往天房者，为真主而朝觐天房，是人民对真主应尽的义务。"

Al-Quran, Surah Ali-Imran (3:97) — 阿里·伊姆兰章

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said (Hadith):

"Verily, al-Islam is founded on five pillars: testifying that there is no god but Allah, establishing prayer, payment of Zakah, fasting Ramadan, and Pilgrimage to the House."

"伊斯兰建立在五大支柱之上：作证除真主外无神；立礼；纳天课；斋月封斋；朝觐天房。"

Sahih Bukhari & Muslim — 布哈里与穆斯林圣训集

Section 2

History & Origin of Hajj

朝觐的历史与起源

The spiritual origins of Hajj trace back thousands of years to Prophet Ibrahim (Abraham) عليه السلام and his family. The present pattern of Hajj was established and purified by Prophet Muhammad ﷺ around 632 CE during his Farewell Pilgrimage. 朝觐的精神起源可追溯到数千年前的先知易卜拉欣（亚伯拉罕）عليه السلام及其家人。现行朝觐形式由先知穆罕默德ﷺ于公元632年的"告别朝觐"中建立并净化。

1

Allah's Command

Allah commanded Prophet Ibrahim to bring his wife Hajar and infant son Ismail to the barren valley of Makkah.

真主命令易卜拉欣先知将其妻子哈吉尔和婴儿伊斯玛仪带到麦加的荒凉峡谷。

2

Miracle of Zamzam 渗渗泉

Hajar ran 7 times between Safa & Marwah searching for water. Allah caused the sacred Zamzam spring to gush forth — commemorated in Sa'i.

哈吉尔在萨法和麦尔沃之间奔跑七次寻找水源。真主使渗渗泉涌现——在赛跑仪式中被纪念。

3

Building the Ka'abah 克尔白

Allah instructed Ibrahim and his son Ismail (now grown) to construct the Ka'abah as the first House of Worship.

真主命令易卜拉欣和其子伊斯玛仪共同建造克尔白，作为第一座礼拜场所（天房）。

4

Test of Sacrifice 牺牲的考验

Ibrahim was tested to sacrifice Ismail. At the last moment, a ram was sent as substitute — commemorated in Eid al-Adha.

易卜拉欣受考验宰杀伊斯玛仪。在最后一刻，一只公羊被派来作为代替——在宰牲节中被纪念。

5

Proclamation of Hajj 宣告朝觐

After building the Ka'abah, Ibrahim received revelation to proclaim pilgrimage to all of mankind.

建造克尔白后，易卜拉欣接受启示，向全人类宣告朝觐的义务。

6

Prophet's Reformation 先知的改革

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ purified Hajj from pre-Islamic pagan practices and restored it to pure monotheism during 632 CE.

先知穆罕默德ﷺ净化了前伊斯兰异教习俗，于公元632年将朝觐恢复为纯粹的一神教形式。

Section 3

Conditions of Hajj (Syarat Wajib)

朝觐的条件（شروط وجوب الحج）

Hajj becomes obligatory when ALL of these conditions are met. 满足所有以下条件时，朝觐成为义务。

# Arabic Term English 中文 Details
1 إسلام Islam 必须是穆斯林 Only obligatory for Muslims
2 عقل 'Aql — Sanity 精神正常，理智健全 Sound mind (not insane)
3 بلوغ Bulugh — Puberty 已达青春期（成年） Must have reached puberty
4 حرية Hurriyyah — Freedom 自由身（非奴隶） Must be a free person
5 استطاعة Istita'ah — Capability 具备能力（体力+财力） Financial & physical ability; safety of journey; Mahram for women

Istita'ah (Capability) includes: Sufficient provisions for travel; ability to support dependents; physical health; and for women — a Mahram (محرم) male guardian to accompany her.

能力（استطاعة）包括：有足够的旅行费用；能够在旅途中维持家人的生活；身体健康；对于女性，还需有男性监护人（马赫兰姆）陪同。

Section 4

Types of Hajj (أنواع الحج)

朝觐的三种类型

Most Recommended
حج التمتع
Hajj al-Tamattu'
享受朝觐

Perform Umrah first during Hajj months, exit Ihram, then re-enter Ihram for Hajj on 8th Dhul-Hijjah. Best for distant pilgrims. Requires a sacrifice (dam).

先进行副朝，之后解除戒戒，在朝觐月八日重新进入戒戒状态。推荐给远方朝觐者。需献祭。

Dam required 需献祭
Combined
حج القِران
Hajj al-Qiran
联合朝觐

Enter Ihram for both Umrah and Hajj together without breaking Ihram. Stay in Ihram throughout. Requires a sacrifice (dam).

同时为副朝和朝觐进入戒戒状态，自始至终不解除戒戒。需献祭。

Dam required 需献祭
Singular
حج الإفراد
Hajj al-Ifrad
单独朝觐

Enter Ihram for Hajj only, no Umrah. Common for residents near Makkah. No sacrifice required.

仅为朝觐进入戒戒状态，不进行副朝，通常适用于麦加附近的居民，无需献祭。

No dam required 无需献祭

Section 5

Pillars of Hajj (أركان الحج)

朝觐的支柱（若遗漏则朝觐无效）

⚠️ Important: If any pillar is missed, the Hajj is INVALID and cannot be compensated by a sacrifice. They must be observed in order.

⚠️ 重要：若遗漏任何支柱，朝觐将无效，且不能以任何祭品弥补。必须按顺序履行。

PILLAR 01
الإحرام
Ihram
戒戒（进入神圣状态）

Forming the intention (niyyah) and entering the sacred state at the Miqat boundary. Wearing white seamless cloth (for men) and reciting Talbiyah.

PILLAR 02 ⭐ Most Critical
الوقوف بعرفة
Wuquf at 'Arafah
站立于阿拉法特

Standing at the plain of 'Arafah on the 9th of Dhul-Hijjah from noon to sunset. The Prophet ﷺ said: "Hajj is 'Arafah." (Ahmad)

PILLAR 03
طواف الإفاضة
Tawaf al-Ifadah
绕行天房（正式环游）

Circumambulating (walking around) the Ka'abah seven times counter-clockwise as the main obligatory Tawaf of Hajj.

PILLAR 04
السعي
Sa'i
赛跑（萨法与麦尔沃之间）

Walking/running seven times between the hills of Safa and Marwah, commemorating Hajar's search for water for baby Ismail.

Section 6

Obligatory Acts — Wajibat (واجبات الحج)

朝觐的义务行为（遗漏需献祭补偿）

Wajibat are required acts. If missed, the Hajj is still valid, but a penalty sacrifice (Dam / دم) must be offered.

朝觐义务行为若被遗漏，朝觐仍然有效，但必须以惩罚性祭品（Dam/血赎）作为补偿。

1
الإحرام من الميقاتEnter Ihram at the Miqat — designated boundary point在指定边界（米卡特）处进入戒戒状态
2
المبيت بمزدلفةMabit in Muzdalifah — overnight stay on 9th night在穆兹达利法过夜（在此收集石子）
3
المبيت بمنىMabit in Mina — overnight stay during Tashreeq nights (11–13 Dhul-Hijjah)在米纳（塔什里克之夜11–13日）过夜
4
رمي الجمراتRami al-Jamarat — stoning all three pillars in Mina向米纳三根石柱投掷石子（投石礼）
5
الحلق أو التقصيرHalq or Taqsir — shaving the head (men) or cutting a lock of hair (women)剃发（男性）或剪一缕头发（女性）
6
طواف الوداعTawaf al-Wada' — Farewell Tawaf before departing Makkah离开麦加前的告别环游天房

Section 7

Step-by-Step Rituals

朝觐仪式逐步指南（日程安排）

8
8th Dhul-Hijjah — Yawm al-Tarwiyah 第一天

Preparation Day 准备日

  • 1
    Enter Ihram — Perform ghusl (ritual bath), wear ihram garments (white seamless cloth for men), form niyyah, recite Talbiyah: "Labbayk Allāhumma Labbayk…"
    进入戒戒状态——进行净浴，穿上朝觐服，做出意图，诵念特比亚颂词
  • 2
    Travel to Mina — Spend the day and night in prayer and worship
    前往米纳——在那里度过白天和夜晚，进行礼拜
9
9th Dhul-Hijjah — Yawm 'Arafah ⭐ 第二天：阿拉法特日

The Heart of Hajj 朝觐核心

  • 3
    Wuquf at 'Arafah — Travel to the plain of 'Arafah; stay from noon to sunset in supplication, dhikr, and prayer; listen to the Hajj sermon (Khutbah); combine Dhuhr and Asr prayers
    在阿拉法特站立——从正午至日落进行祈祷、赞念；聆听朝觐布道词；合并晌礼与晡礼
  • 4
    Travel to Muzdalifah — After sunset; spend the night under the open sky; collect pebbles for stoning; pray Maghrib and Isha combined
    日落后前往穆兹达利法——在露天过夜；收集石子；合并昏礼和宵礼
10
10th Dhul-Hijjah — Eid al-Adha 🐑 宰牲节

Day of Sacrifice 献祭日

  • 5
    Rami — Stone the large pillar (Jamrat al-'Aqabah) 7 times in Mina
    投石礼——向最大的雅各白石柱投掷七颗石子
  • 6
    Nahr (Sacrifice/Qurbani) — Slaughter an animal in remembrance of Prophet Ibrahim's sacrifice
    宰牲——宰杀牲畜，纪念先知易卜拉欣的牺牲精神
  • 7
    Halq or Taqsir — Shave the head (men) or cut a lock of hair (women)
    剃发或剪发——男性剃头，女性剪一缕头发
  • 8
    Tawaf al-Ifadah — Main obligatory circumambulation of the Ka'abah (7 rounds counter-clockwise)
    正式绕行天房（逆时针环游七圈）
  • 9
    Sa'i — Walk/run 7 times between the hills of Safa and Marwah
    在萨法和麦尔沃之间奔走七次
⋯
11th–13th Dhul-Hijjah — Ayyam al-Tashreeq 塔什里克之日

Days of Remembrance 纪念之日

  • 10
    Mabit in Mina — Spend nights in Mina; days for worship and supplication
    在米纳过夜，白天进行礼拜和祈祷
  • 11
    Rami al-Jamarat — Stone ALL three pillars (small, medium, large) — 7 pebbles each, on 11th, 12th and optionally 13th
    向三根石柱（小、中、大）各投掷七颗石子——十一、十二日（可含十三日）
  • 12
    Tawaf al-Wada' — Farewell Tawaf (circumambulate Ka'abah 7 times) before departing Makkah
    离开麦加前进行告别环游天房（七圈）

Section 9

Spiritual Significance & Benefits

朝觐的精神意义与益处

🌿
Forgiveness of Sins
罪孽的宽恕

"Whoever performs Hajj and does not sin will return home as sinless as the day his mother gave birth to him." (Sahih Bukhari)

🌙
Attaining Jannah
获得天园的奖励

"The reward for an accepted Hajj is nothing less than Paradise." (Sahih Bukhari)

🤝
Unity of the Ummah
穆斯林民族的统一

Muslims from all races, nationalities, and classes gather as equals, wearing the same white Ihram garments — symbolizing brotherhood.

🙏
Submission & Humility
顺从与谦逊

Hajj reinforces total submission to Allah's will and cultivates deep humility and gratitude in the pilgrim's heart.

💎
Spiritual Purification
精神净化

Hajj serves as a powerful training course cultivating self-restraint, patience, and moral character through its rigorous rituals.

⚖️
Reminder of the Hereafter
对后世的提醒

The rituals remind pilgrims of the Day of Judgment — the vast gathering at 'Arafah symbolizes standing before Allah equally.

Interactive Quiz

互动测验 — Test Your Knowledge

Q1. Hajj is the _____ Pillar of Islam.

Q1. 朝觐是伊斯兰的第_____功。

✅ Correct! Hajj is the Fifth and final Pillar of Islam. 正确！朝觐是伊斯兰的第五功，也是最后一功。

Q2. Which pillar of Hajj is considered the MOST critical, where the Prophet ﷺ said "Hajj is ______"?

Q2. 先知ﷺ说"朝觐就是_____"，哪一个支柱被认为最关键？

✅ Correct! "Al-Hajju 'Arafah" — The Prophet ﷺ said Hajj is 'Arafah. Standing there is the most critical pillar. 正确！先知ﷺ说"朝觐就是阿拉法特"，在阿拉法特站立是最关键的支柱。

Q3. What is the difference between a Pillar (Rukn) and a Wajib of Hajj?

Q3. 朝觐的支柱（Rukn）和义务（Wajib）有何区别？

✅ Correct! Missing a Pillar makes Hajj invalid with no compensation. Missing a Wajib keeps Hajj valid but requires offering a Dam (sacrifice). 正确！遗漏支柱使朝觐无效且无法补偿。遗漏义务则朝觐仍有效，但需献上血赎（祭品）。

Q4. Which type of Hajj is MOST recommended for pilgrims coming from distant countries?

Q4. 哪种朝觐类型最推荐给来自远方国家的朝觐者？

✅ Correct! Hajj al-Tamattu' is most recommended for pilgrims from distant lands as it allows rest between Umrah and Hajj. 正确！享受朝觐（Tamattu'）最推荐给远方朝觐者，因为它允许在副朝和朝觐之间有休息时间。

Q5. The Sa'i ritual commemorates whose search for water for her infant child?

Q5. 赛跑仪式纪念的是谁为其婴儿寻找水源？

✅ Correct! Hajar ran 7 times between Safa and Marwah searching for water for her infant son Ismail. Allah caused the Zamzam spring to flow. 正确！哈吉尔在萨法和麦尔沃之间奔跑七次，为其婴儿伊斯玛仪寻找水源。真主使渗渗泉涌现。
