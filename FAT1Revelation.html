🌙 The First Revelation (Wahyu) 🌙

第一次启示 (Wahyu)

Why Do We Live in This World?
🌍
Our Purpose
We live in this world to know Allah and follow His guidance. Allah is the Creator of everything.
我们在这个世界上生活是为了认识真主（安拉）并遵循他的指导。真主是万物的创造者。
❓
How Do We Know?
We know our purpose through guidance. Allah sends prophets to teach us what is right and wrong.
我们通过指导来了解我们的目的。真主派先知来教导我们什么是正确的，什么是错误的。
📢
Allah's Messengers
Allah sent many prophets to different people. For us, Allah sent Prophet Muhammad (SAW) as the last and final prophet.
真主向不同的民族派遣了许多先知。对我们来说，真主派遣了先知穆罕默德(SAW)作为最后和最终的先知。
Who is Prophet Muhammad?
⭐
The Last Prophet
Prophet Muhammad is the last prophet sent by Allah. No prophet will come after him.
穆罕默德先知是真主派遣的最后一位先知。在他之后不会再有先知来临。
🙏
What is a Prophet?
A prophet is a chosen man among people chosen by Allah to give guidance. Without a prophet, we cannot know what is true and what is wrong.
先知是真主在人类中选择的人，被选中来提供指导。没有先知，我们无法知道什么是真实的，什么是错误的。
✨
Miracles
Allah gives miracles to prophets to prove they speak the truth. A miracle is something that shows Allah's power.
真主给予先知奇迹来证明他们说的是真理。奇迹是展示真主力量的东西。
Prophet Muhammad's Early Life
📍
Where Was He Born?
Prophet Muhammad was born in Mecca, Arabia. His father Abdullah died before he was even born.
先知穆罕默德出生在阿拉伯的麦加。他的父亲阿卜杜拉在他出生前就已去世。
📅
When Was He Born?
Prophet Muhammad was born on Monday, 12 Rabi al-Awwal in the year 570. He was an orphan from birth.
先知穆罕默德在公元 570 年的阿拉伯月12日（星期一）出生。他从出生就是孤儿。
💔
His Mother
His mother Amina died when he was only 2 years old. He grew up without his parents.
他的母亲阿米娜在他只有2岁时去世。他在没有父母的情况下长大。
The First Revelation (The Encounter with Angel Jibreel)
🏔️
The Cave of Hira
When Prophet Muhammad grew up, he loved to be alone and think about life. He went to a cave called Gua Hira to meditate.
先知穆罕默德长大后，喜欢独处和思考生活。他去了一个叫希拉山洞的地方进行冥想。
😨
The Fearful Vision
While in the cave, Prophet Muhammad suddenly saw Angel Jibreel (Gabriel) in his true form. The angel filled the entire horizon. This made him very afraid.
在山洞里，先知穆罕默德突然看到了真正形态的吉卜里天使（加百列）。天使充满整个地平线。这让他非常害怕。
📖
The Command: Iqra!
The angel gave a command: "Iqra!" which means "Read!" Prophet Muhammad replied: "I cannot read" because he was unlettered (could not read or write).
天使下令："伊克拉！"意思是"读！"先知穆罕默德回答："我不会读"，因为他文盲(不会读或写)。
🤗
The Embrace
Then the angel held him very tightly three times, then released him. Each time, the angel commanded him to read again until he could.
然后天使非常紧紧地拥抱他三次，然后放开他。每一次，天使都命令他再读一次，直到他能够。
The First Verses of the Quran

Surah Al-Alaq (96:1-5)

اقْرَأْ بِاسْمِ رَبِّكَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ
خَلَقَ الْإِنسَانَ مِنْ عَلَقٍ
اقْرَأْ وَرَبُّكَ الْأَكْرَمُ
الَّذِي عَلَّمَ بِالْقَلَمِ
عَلَّمَ الْإِنسَانَ مَا لَمْ يَعْلَمْ
Translation:

"Read in the name of your Lord who created—
Created man from a clinging substance.
Read, and your Lord is the most Generous—
Who taught by the pen—
Taught man that which he knew not."
中文翻译：

"奉你的创造你的主的名义而诵读——
他用血块创造了人。
你诵读，你的主是最慷慨的——
他用笔教人——
教人以前所不知道的。"
Prophet Muhammad Returns Home
🏃
He Ran Home
Trembling with fear, Prophet Muhammad quickly ran home to his wife Khadijah.
先知穆罕默德害怕地颤抖，迅速跑回家去见他的妻子哈迪彦。
👩‍🤝‍👨
Khadijah's Support
He said to her: "Cover me! Cover me!" Khadijah comforted and reassured him. She was very supportive and kind.
他对她说："给我盖上！给我盖上！"哈迪彦安慰和向他保证。她非常支持他和善良。
📜
Waraqah's Confirmation
Khadijah took him to her cousin Waraqah ibn Nawfal, a learned Christian. Waraqah said: "This is the angel who came to Moses. You are the Prophet of this nation!"
哈迪彦带他去见她的表亲瓦拉卡·伊本·瑙法勒，一位博学的基督徒。瓦拉卡说："这是来见摩西的天使。你是这个民族的先知！"
