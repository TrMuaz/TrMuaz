Zakat al-Mal vs. Zakat al-Fitrah

Both Zakat al-Mal and Zakat al-Fitrah are types of zakat (obligatory charity) in Islam, but they are different in many ways.
天课·财富 (Zakat al-Mal) 和天课·天赋 (Zakat al-Fitrah) 都是伊斯兰教中的天课（必缴慈善），但它们在许多方面有所不同。

Part 2 · 第二部分

What is Zakat al-Fitrah? 什么是开斋天课？

Zakat al-Fitrah is a special type of charity that every Muslim must give at the end of the month of Ramadan.
天课天赋是每位穆斯林必须在斋月结束时缴纳的一种特殊慈善。
Tiānkè tiānfù shì měi wèi mùsīlín bìxū zài zhāiyuè jiéshù shí jiǎonà de yī zhǒng tèshū císhàn.
The word Fitrah (فطرة) means "natural state" or "the breaking of the fast". It reminds us of our duty to help others even during celebrations.
"Fitrah"这个词的意思是"自然本性"或"开斋"。它提醒我们，即使在庆祝活动中也有责任帮助他人。
"Fitrah" zhège cí de yìsi shì "zìrán běnxìng" huò "kāizhāi". Tā tíxǐng wǒmen, jíshǐ zài qìngzhù huódòng zhōng yě yǒu zérèn bāngzhù tārén.
It was made obligatory (wajib) by the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ in the second year of Hijrah, the same year that fasting in Ramadan was ordained.
先知穆罕默德ﷺ在伊斯兰历第二年规定了它为义务，与规定斋月封斋的同年。
Xiānzhī Mùhǎnmòdéﷺ zài yīsīlán lì dì èr nián guīdìng le tā wéi yìwù, yǔ guīdìng zhāiyuè fēngzhāi de tóngnián.
📌 Remember / 记住 (Jì zhù):
Zakat al-Fitrah is paid by the head of the family on behalf of every family member — including babies and elderly relatives. 开斋天课由家长代表每位家庭成员缴纳，包括婴儿和老年亲属。
Kāizhāi tiānkè yóu jiāzhǎng dàibiǎo měi wèi jiātíng chéngyuán jiǎonà, bāokuò yīng'ér hé lǎonián qīnshǔ.
Part 3 · 第三部分

Ruling (Hukm) 法律地位 (Fǎlǜ dìwèi)

Zakat al-Fitrah is Fardhu 'Ain — it is an individual obligation. This means every capable Muslim must pay it personally.
开斋天课是"个人义务"（Fardhu 'Ain）——这意味着每位有能力的穆斯林必须亲自缴纳。
Kāizhāi tiānkè shì "gèrén yìwù" (Fardhu 'Ain) — zhè yìwèizhe měi wèi yǒu nénglì de mùsīlín bìxū qīnzì jiǎonà.

Who must pay? 谁必须缴纳？

A Muslim must pay Zakat al-Fitrah if they: (1) are Muslim, (2) are alive at the end of Ramadan, and (3) have enough food for themselves and family on Eid day.
穆斯林必须缴纳开斋天课的条件：(1) 是穆斯林，(2) 在斋月结束时仍然在世，(3) 在开斋节当天有足够的食物供自己和家人食用。
Mùsīlín bìxū jiǎonà kāizhāi tiānkè de tiáojiàn: (1) shì mùsīlín, (2) zài zhāiyuè jiéshù shí réngrán zàishì, (3) zài kāizhāi jié dāngtiān yǒu zúgòu de shíwù gōng zìjǐ hé jiārén shíyòng.
💡 Did you know? / 你知道吗？(Nǐ zhīdào ma?)
Even a baby born before sunset on the last day of Ramadan must have Zakat al-Fitrah paid for them by their parents! 即使是在斋月最后一天日落前出生的婴儿，其父母也必须为他们缴纳开斋天课！
Jíshǐ shì zài zhāiyuè zuìhòu yītiān rìluò qián chūshēng de yīng'ér, qí fùmǔ yě bìxū wèi tāmen jiǎonà kāizhāi tiānkè!
Part 4 · 第四部分

Amount & Type 数量与种类 (Shùliàng yǔ zhǒnglèi)

The amount of Zakat al-Fitrah is one sa' of food — which equals approximately 2.5 kg to 3 kg of the staple food in your area.
开斋天课的数量是一个"萨"（Sa'）的食物——约等于你所在地区的主食2.5至3公斤。
Kāizhāi tiānkè de shùliàng shì yīgè "sà" (Sa') de shíwù — yuē děngyú nǐ suǒzài dìqū de zhǔshí 2.5 zhì 3 gōngjīn.
In Malaysia, the staple food is rice. So each person must pay approximately 2.7 kg of rice, or its equivalent in money (as determined by the local Islamic authority).
在马来西亚，主食是大米。因此每人必须缴纳约2.7公斤的大米，或其等值金额（由当地伊斯兰当局规定）。
Zài mǎláixīyà, zhǔshí shì dàmǐ. Yīncǐ měi rén bìxū jiǎonà yuē 2.7 gōngjīn de dàmǐ, huò qí děngzhí jīn'é (yóu dāngdì yīsīlán dāngjú guīdìng).
Today, most Islamic authorities allow payment in money (cash) instead of food, to make it easier and more useful for the poor.
如今，大多数伊斯兰当局允许用金钱（现金）代替食物缴纳，以便为穷人提供更方便、更实用的帮助。
Rújīn, dàduōshù yīsīlán dāngjú yǔnxǔ yòng jīnqián (xiànjīn) dàitì shíwù jiǎonà, yǐbiàn wèi qióngrén tígōng gèng fāngbiàn, gèng shíyòng de bāngzhù.
Part 5 · 第五部分

When & Who Receives It? 何时缴？谁接受？

Time of Payment 缴纳时间

There are different time categories for paying Zakat al-Fitrah. The best time is before the Eid prayer on the morning of Eid al-Fitr.
缴纳开斋天课有不同的时间类别。最佳时间是在开斋节早上的节礼拜前。
Jiǎonà kāizhāi tiānkè yǒu bùtóng de shíjiān lèibié. Zuì jiā shíjiān shì zài kāizhāi jié zǎoshang de jié lǐbài qián.
  • 1
    Permitted (Harus) — From the 1st of Ramadan. You may pay early. 允许（Harus）— 从斋月第一天起。可以提前缴纳。
    Yǔnxǔ — cóng zhāiyuè dì yī tiān qǐ. Kěyǐ tíqián jiǎonà.
  • 2
    Recommended (Sunat) — From sunset of the last night of Ramadan. 推荐（Sunat）— 从斋月最后一夜的日落开始。
    Tuījiàn — cóng zhāiyuè zuìhòu yī yè de rìluò kāishǐ.
  • 3
    Obligatory Deadline (Wajib) — Before Eid prayer on Eid al-Fitr. 义务截止日期（Wajib）— 开斋节礼拜前。
    Yìwù jiézhǐ rìqī — kāizhāi jié lǐbài qián.
  • 4
    Makruh (Disliked) — After Eid prayer but still on Eid day. 可憎（Makruh）— 开斋节礼拜后，但仍在开斋节当天。
    Kězēng — kāizhāi jié lǐbài hòu, dàn réng zài kāizhāi jié dāngtiān.
  • 5
    Haram (Forbidden) — After Eid day. It becomes a debt you must repay! 禁止（Haram）— 开斋节后。它变成你必须偿还的债务！
    Jìnzhǐ — kāizhāi jié hòu. Tā biàn chéng nǐ bìxū chánghuán de zhàiwù!

Who Receives Zakat al-Fitrah? 谁接受开斋天课？

Zakat al-Fitrah is distributed to Asnaf — 8 groups of people who are eligible to receive zakat, as stated in the Quran (Surah at-Tawbah 9:60).
开斋天课分配给"Asnaf"——《古兰经》（第9章第60节）中提到的8类有资格接受天课的人。
Kāizhāi tiānkè fēnpèi gěi "Asnaf" — gǔlán jīng (dì 9 zhāng dì 60 jié) zhōng tídào de 8 lèi yǒu zīgé jiēshòu tiānkè de rén.
The most important group for Zakat al-Fitrah is the Fakir (very poor) and Miskin (poor), so they can celebrate Eid with food and happiness.
开斋天课最重要的接受群体是"Fakir"（极度贫困者）和"Miskin"（贫困者），使他们能够在开斋节享有食物和快乐。
Kāizhāi tiānkè zuì zhòngyào de jiēshòu qúntǐ shì "Fakir" (jídù pínkùn zhě) hé "Miskin" (pínkùn zhě), shǐ tāmen nénggòu zài kāizhāi jié xiǎngyǒu shíwù hé kuàilè.
🌙 Wisdom / 智慧 (Zhìhuì):
The Prophet ﷺ said: "Feed them (the poor) on this day (Eid) so they do not have to beg from people." This shows that Zakat al-Fitrah is about dignity for all. 先知ﷺ说："在这一天（开斋节）喂养穷人，使他们不必向人乞讨。"这表明开斋天课是关于人人有尊严的。
Xiānzhī shuō: "Zài zhè yītiān wèiyǎng qióngrén, shǐ tāmen bù bì xiàng rén qǐtǎo." Zhè biǎomíng kāizhāi tiānkè shì guānyú rénrén yǒu zūnyán de.
