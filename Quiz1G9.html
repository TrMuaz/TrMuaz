In Islamic law, fasting means to intentionally refrain from food, drink and invalidators from sunrise until sunset for the sake of Allah. 在伊斯兰教法中，斋戒是指为了安拉而从日出到日落，有意识地远离饮食和一切会使斋戒无效的事物。
Kaffarah fasting is a voluntary fast that a Muslim can do anytime to get more reward. 赎罪斋（Kaffarah）是一种自愿性斋戒，穆斯林可以在任何时间为获取更多回赐而进行。
The time to make the intention (niyyah) for an obligatory fast is during the night, from Maghrib until before Fajr. 为履行主命斋而立意（niyyah）的时间是在夜间，从昏礼（Maghrib）之后到晨礼（Fajr）之前。
Accidental vomiting does not break the fast, but intentionally making yourself vomit does. 无意呕吐不会使斋戒无效，但故意让自己呕吐则会破坏斋戒。
Fasting for health reasons is not permissible and is sinful; a Muslim must never think about health when fasting and should only be sincere to Allah. 为了健康而斋戒是不被允许的，是有罪的；穆斯林在斋戒时绝不能想到健康，只应当只为安拉而真诚。
Taraweeh prayer in Ramadan is highly recommended (sunnah mu’akkadah), not compulsory. 在斋月里的台拉维哈（Taraweeh）拜是非常被强调的圣行（sunnah mu’akkadah），但并非强制性的。
Muslims should eat suhoor as early as possible and delay iftar as late as possible in order to gain more reward from Allah. 穆斯林应该尽量在很早的时候吃封斋饭（suhoor），并尽量推迟开斋（iftar），以从安拉那里获得更多的回赐。
A traveller (generally 89 km or more) is allowed to break the fast and doesn't need to make up the missed days (qada) later. 旅行者（通常为 89 公里或以上）被允许开斋，并且不需要日后补还所缺的斋日（qada）。
