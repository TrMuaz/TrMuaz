Zakat al-Fitrah

Lesson Notes · 开斋天课学习笔记

Part 1 · 第一部分

What is Zakat al-Fitrah? 什么是开斋天课？

Zakat al-Fitrah is a special type of charity that every Muslim must give at the end of the month of Ramadan.

天课天赋是每位穆斯林必须在斋月结束时缴纳的一种特殊慈善。

The word Fitrah (فطرة) means “natural state” or “the breaking of the fast”. It reminds us to help others even during celebrations.

“Fitrah”这个词的意思是“自然本性”或“开斋”。它提醒我们，即使在庆祝时也要帮助他人。

It was made obligatory (wajib) by the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ in the second year of Hijrah, the same year fasting in Ramadan was ordained.

先知穆罕默德 ﷺ 在伊斯兰历第二年规定开斋天课为义务，与规定斋月封斋是同一年。

Feature 项目 Zakat al-Mal 财富天课 Zakat al-Fitrah 开斋天课

Part 2 · 第二部分

Who Needs to Pay? 谁必须缴纳？

Zakat al-Fitrah is Fardhu ‘Ain, an individual obligation. Every capable Muslim must pay it personally or through the head of the family.

开斋天课是个人义务（Fardhu ‘Ain）。每位有能力的穆斯林都必须缴纳，可以由家长代为缴纳。

A Muslim must pay Zakat al-Fitrah if:

一名穆斯林必须缴纳开斋天课，如果他符合以下条件：

  • They are Muslim.
    他是穆斯林。
  • They are alive at the end of Ramadan.
    在斋月结束时仍然在世。
  • They have enough food for themselves and their family on the day of Eid.
    在开斋节当天有足够的食物供自己和家人食用。

Example: Even a baby born before sunset on the last day of Ramadan must have Zakat al-Fitrah paid for them by their parents.

例子：即使是在斋月最后一天日落前出生的婴儿，其父母也必须为他缴纳开斋天课。

Part 3 · 第三部分

When Can We Pay? 何时可以缴？

There are several time categories for paying Zakat al-Fitrah. The best time is before the Eid al-Fitr prayer on the morning of Eid.

缴纳开斋天课有几个时间类别。最佳时间是在开斋节早上的节礼拜之前。

Time Categories 缴纳时间类别

  • Permitted (Harus): From the first day of Ramadan until the end of Ramadan.
    允许时间：从斋月第一天到斋月结束。
  • Obligatory (Wajib): When the sun sets on the last day of Ramadan.
    义务时间：在斋月最后一天日落时。
  • Best time (Sunat / Afdal): On the morning of Eid al-Fitr, after sunrise and before the Eid prayer.
    最佳时间：在开斋节早晨，从日出后至开斋节礼拜前。
  • Makruh (Disliked): After the Eid prayer but before sunset on the first day of Shawwal.
    可憎时间：在开斋节礼拜之后至肖瓦尔月第一天日落之前。
  • Haram (Forbidden): After sunset on the first day of Shawwal; paying after this time is considered a late payment that must still be repaid.
    禁止时间：在肖瓦尔月第一天日落之后，此时才缴属于拖欠，变成必须补缴的“欠债”。
Part 4 · 第四部分

How Do We Pay? 如何缴纳？

The amount of Zakat al-Fitrah is one sa’ of food, which is about 2.5–3 kg of the staple food in your area.

开斋天课的数量是一个“萨”（sa’）的食物，大约等于当地主食的 2.5 至 3 公斤。

In Malaysia, the staple food is rice, so each person must pay about 2.7 kg of rice or its cash equivalent as decided by the state Islamic council.

在马来西亚，主食是大米，因此每人必须缴纳约 2.7 公斤大米，或由州伊斯兰理事会规定的等值现金。

Today, many Islamic authorities allow payment in money (cash) instead of food, to make it easier and more useful for the poor.

如今，许多伊斯兰当局允许用现金代替食物缴纳，以便为穷人提供更方便、更实用的帮助。

Who Receives It? 谁可以接受？

Zakat al-Fitrah is given to the Asnaf, the eight categories of people who can receive zakat, especially the poor (fakir and miskin) so that they can celebrate Eid with dignity.

开斋天课给“Asnaf”——有资格接受天课的八类人，特别是贫穷者（Fakir 和 Miskin），使他们也能有尊严地庆祝开斋节。

Part 5 · 第五部分

Why Do We Pay? 为什么要缴？

Zakat al-Fitrah helps to purify our fasting from small mistakes, such as bad words or wasting time during Ramadan.

开斋天课可以净化我们的斋戒，弥补我们在斋月中犯下的小错误，例如说坏话或浪费时间。

It also helps the poor to enjoy Eid so that they do not need to beg on that day.

它还可以帮助穷人，让他们在开斋节这一天也能有食物，不必向人乞讨。

Wisdom: The aim is that every Muslim can feel joy, have food, and maintain dignity on Eid, not only the rich.

智慧：其目标是让每一位穆斯林在开斋节都能感受到快乐、拥有食物并保持尊严，而不只是富人。

Quick Quiz 小测验

Answer the questions below to check your understanding.

回答以下问题来检查你的理解程度。

1. Who must pay Zakat al-Fitrah?

  1. Only rich adult Muslims.
  2. Every Muslim, including children (through their guardian).
  3. Only those who fasted in Ramadan.

2. What is the best time to pay Zakat al-Fitrah?

  1. After the Eid prayer.
  2. Before the Eid prayer on the morning of Eid al-Fitr.
  3. The day after Eid.

3. Approximately how much is one sa’ of food?

  1. About 500 grams.
  2. About 10 kilograms.
  3. About 2.5–3 kilograms.
